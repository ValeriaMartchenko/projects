Paper Laptops

Paper laptops have always been the best way to take notes, learn, and have fun without an electronic computer, the only problem was finding authentic quality made ones for cheaper than 5$, at creativity paper laptops, we create artisanal, fully handmade, paper laptops to improve your school and work experience. Our owner is an experience paper laptop artisan and has over 4 years of experience.

Our paper laptops are no-nonsense, while they are made of paper, that doesn't mean we put on a keyboard and pretend to type on it, these are made to be reusable infinitely and serve an actual function rather than just a toy, along with making school less boring.

In fact, we are so confident in our products, If you are ever not satisfied with your paper laptop, just hand it back in for a full refund as long as it is withing 7 days, and in good condition.
